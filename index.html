About the Advocate

Committed to Justice, Dedicated to You

Advocate Aditya Vinayak Dike is a practicing lawyer based in Satara, providing legal assistance in civil, family, and consumer disputes. He is committed to delivering practical legal solutions with integrity and professionalism.

He completed his law degree from Dr. D. Y. Patil Law College, Pimpri Chinchwad, Pune, and has successfully cleared the All India Bar Examination (AIBE).

With a focused approach and dedication to client needs, he assists individuals and families in resolving disputes efficiently and effectively.
